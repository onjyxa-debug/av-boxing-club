About the Gym

Built in the Heart of the AV

AV Boxing Club is a community-driven boxing gym located in the heart of Palmdale, California. Built from the strong boxing culture of the Antelope Valley, the club develops athletes at every level — from beginners learning their first jab to competitive fighters preparing for amateur bouts.

The gym provides a supportive, disciplined environment where members improve their physical fitness, learn self-defense, and build real confidence through the sport of boxing. Like the legendary Wild Card in Hollywood, we believe boxing belongs to everyone — and we make it for the Antelope Valley.

We serve Palmdale, Lancaster, and the surrounding communities. If you're in the AV and you want to box — this is your gym.

Programs

Every Level, Every Goal

01

Beginner

No experience needed. Learn stance, footwork, basic combinations, and ring awareness from day one.

02

Intermediate

Build on your foundation with advanced combos, pad work, defensive movement, and coach-supervised sparring.

03

Competition

Full fight prep for USA Boxing sanctioned events. If you want to compete in the AV and beyond — this is the program.

04

Fitness Boxing

Real boxing training for fitness. Burn fat, build strength, sharpen your mind — without ever stepping in the ring.
